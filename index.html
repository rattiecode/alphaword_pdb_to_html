AlphaWord PDB Encoder/Decoder

This window can convert PDB files to Markdown. Right now, only bold and italic formatting is retained. This project is a work-in-progress! In the future, we plan to add functionality to convert Markdown back to PDB.

Upload a .PDB file, and it will be converted to Markdown and appear in the text boxes below.

File conversion is not processed by a server; it is handled within your browser window. Your text stays 100% in your browser and is never sent to us.
